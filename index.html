SUBJECT PRESENTATION AND ANALYSIS:

The modern theory of Markowitz tells us that building a diversified portfolio of assets is the key to minimize the risks related to an investment. To do so, the portfolio must be composed of assets that react differently to the same events. That is why it is essential to demonstrate the dependency relationship between those assets.
Common approaches to portfolio optimization require classical correlation measurements (such as Spearman or Pearson methods) which are not appropriate to model the dependency between the yields.

That’s why we need copula in order to have better models. Thus, our goal is to optimize a portfolio using an estimated parameter that we have to find thanks to copulas.

WHAT'S NEXT?

We are now going to read and work on the different documents given by our partner, in order to be able to create a portfolio that is efficient according to the Markowitz’s modern portfolio theory.

When that will be done, we will compute the different risk measures of the portfolio (Value-At- Risk, Conditional Value-At-Risk etc...). But all the work we have done until now is done with variance-covariance measures. The problem with the use of this method, is that in case of an extreme scenario, the response of our portfolio with variance-covariance won’t be representative of the reality as the relationship between asset will become asymmetrical during those scenarios.

As explained by our partner, it is important to highlight the dependence relationship between financial asset that are well represented, as the portfolio optimisation is made according to the correlation measures between assets.

Our final aim is to do the same work with copulas. In fact, we started by creating a diverse portfolio in the meaning of variance-covariance measures. We will be working with copulas, that are a multivariate cumulative distribution function for which the marginal probability distribution of each variable is uniform. That means that copulas are a mathematical instrument that allow us to create a diverse portfolio that will support a crisis or an intense phenomenon. When using copulas, we can represent the dependence relationships without it being distorted. We will have then more adequate relationships between assets, and therefore a more optimised portfolio.

Therefore, we will re-create out portfolio by computing the correlation between assets with copulas, make a copula function which is bi-varied for each couple of assets, and by calculating the couple return-risk of our portfolio. We are then going to estimate de dependence matrix of our portfolio with statistics tests and copulas method, in order to build the dependence matrix of the portfolio. After that, we will then proceed to the optimization of the couple risk-return of the portfolio, in order to minimize as much as possible, the risk of our portfolio according to the dependence measures.
